GDP per capita and Suicide rate

In the recent years, there has been an increasing number of suicides among the youths. One main reason highlighted is poor standards of living or lack of money. The below visualisations tries to explore the concern. In the analysis, GDP per capita can be taken as a rough estimate for the standard of living and a normalised value for suicide rates among 100,000 people can be taken.

Top and Bottom 3 GDP per Capita countries and their Suicide rates

The image rather shows a controversial finding where the top 3 countires based GDP per capita (Luxembourg, Norway and Switzerland) have higher suicide rates as compared to the bottom 3 countires based on GDP per Capita (Togo, Uganda and Chad)
